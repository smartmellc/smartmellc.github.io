About us (page in progress)

After four generations, Smart Method Enterprises is the current evolution of the Smart family. Taylor Smart, the sole proprietor of SME, was was born and raised in the western Washington area, and continues to give back to the Greater Seattle area. SME is dedicated to providing quality construction while utilizing only local subcontractors and suppliers to guarantee that work done here benefits everyone here. The Smart Method philosphy is that the job is only completed when the client sees their dream gain physical form.
